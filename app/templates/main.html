Public project

lovspor / lovverk

An open project for working with Norwegian legal text, in two parts.

lovspor is the engine — a Python pipeline that converts Norwegian legal open data into clean, structured Markdown.

lovverk is the destination — a place where non-technical readers can browse and download those laws as ready-to-use Markdown, no scripting required.

The goal is to make legal text easier to search, version, inspect, and use in LLM-assisted workflows without losing document structure.
